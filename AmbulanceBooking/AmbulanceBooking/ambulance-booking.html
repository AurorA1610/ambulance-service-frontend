Choose Ambulance Category

Basic

Basic life support ambulances are emergency ambulances and aid the necessary medical support during the transit till the patient is transported to the hospital. It will be well equipped with basic medical services like:

  • Blood pressure monitoring equipment
  • Pulse Oximetry
  • Oxygen

Advanced

Advanced life support ambulance has:

  • A paramedic on board
  • An emergency medical technician
  • Cardiac life support
  • Cardiac monitors
  • Glucose-testing device

Personnel are allowed administer medications, and give injections to help stabilize the patient on the way to a nearby health centre.
