Choose Ambulance Category

Basic

Basic life support ambulances are emergency ambulances and aid the necessary medical support during the transit till the patient is transported to the hospital it will be well equipped with basic medical services like:

• Blood pressure monitoring equipment
• Pulse Oximetry
• Oxygen

Advanced

Advanced life support ambulance has a paramedic on board, along with an emergency medical technician, ambulance is equipped with cardiac life support, cardiac monitors as well as a glucose-testing device,etc. Personnel are allowed administer medications, and give injections to help stabilize the patient on the way to a nearby health centre.
